In DIWALI

There’s palpable excitement among us Indians during the festive season. Most of us plan our big purchases around this period and that stands especially true for car buyers. But why exactly do so many car buyers defer or align their purchases with this festive timeline? For one, it’s considered an auspicious time to bring home a new car. More importantly, Diwali is also a time when carmakers offer attractive deals to entice buyers. But should you be mesmerised by Diwali offers or does it make better financial sense to wait till December when carmakers again offer deep discounts to clear their inventory before the new model year stock arrives?
